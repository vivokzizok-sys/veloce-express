# Veloce Express Terms

Users must provide accurate account, phone, pickup, and drop-off information.

Clients are responsible for accepting a bid only when they agree to the delivery price and request details.

Drivers are responsible for submitting realistic bids, communicating clearly, and confirming delivery only after the item has been handed to the client.

Admins may approve, block, review, or reject accounts and support tickets to protect the service.

These terms are a starter template and should be reviewed before public commercial launch.
