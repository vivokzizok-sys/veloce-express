# Veloce Express Privacy Policy

Veloce Express stores the information needed to operate delivery requests: name, email, phone number, role, orders, bids, ratings, support tickets, and account approval data.

Driver vehicle photos are stored as compressed Base64 data in Firestore because Firebase Storage is not used in this version.

Location data may be stored for active trip operation and is visible only to the matched delivery parties and admins where needed for operations.

Users can contact support or report issues from inside the app. Admins can review these tickets to protect users and improve service quality.

This policy should be reviewed by a legal professional before public commercial launch.
